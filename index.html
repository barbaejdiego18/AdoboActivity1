FILIPINO ADOBO RECIPE

Description

Pork adobo is, inarguably, a Filipino classic. When you think of Filipino cuisine, very few things come to mind before a yummy, juicy pork belly in a savory sauce. This version of the classic adobo dish is one that is saucy yet dry, more concerned with the quality of the sauce than the quantity of it. This Nagmamantikang Pork Adobo is so delicious, you and your family will not be able to get enough! Hopefully, you will still have some to enjoy for leftovers the next day.

Cooking Instructions

  1. Combine the pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic then marinade for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat the pot and put-in the marinated pork belly then cook for a few minutes
  3. Add whole pepper corn, and dried bay leaves then bring to a boil. then simmer.
  4. Then Add water then boil up to 20-30 minutes
  5. Put-in the vinegar and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes
  6. Add salt to taste
  7. Serve when hot. ENJOY!
